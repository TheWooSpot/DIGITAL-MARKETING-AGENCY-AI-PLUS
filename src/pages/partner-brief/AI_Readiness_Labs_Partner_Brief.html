What AI Readiness Labs™ Is

A Four-Rung Journey From AI-Absent to AI-Governed

AI Readiness Labs is a separately priced, deliverable service product — not a backend diagnostic system. The four rungs are distinct client experiences with real deliverables, real pricing, and real outcomes. A prospect does not buy the assessment — they take it free and are placed into the rung that matches their score. What they purchase is the rung experience itself.

Where Your Eyes Are Needed

The Areas Open for Your Input

The framework is solid. The curriculum is mapped. What's still ahead is the human experience of each rung — the content, the delivery, the feeling of being inside the program. That's where your perspective matters most.

Rung 2 — Course architecture and the self-guided learning experience
Five modules, fifteen sessions, ninety days. The question is whether it's compelling enough to keep someone moving — especially someone who just received a score that surprised them. Curriculum flow, session titles, homework design, and the moment-by-moment student experience are open for full critique.
Rung 3 — Workshop format, session flow, and the advisor dynamic
Done-with-you. An advisor in the room, building live, holding the client accountable to a number. What does a great session actually feel like? What's the right cadence and ratio of strategy to hands-on work? The session arc is defined — the texture is not.
Rung 4 — The scope and feel of a long-term managed AI relationship
Technical installation, maintenance, monitoring, and support — ongoing. What does a great stewardship relationship look like month to month? What would make a client renew without thinking twice?
Copy and content across all rungs
Session titles, outcome statements, enrollment language, and email sequences are placeholder-level at this stage. The language that makes a cautious business owner say yes — that needs fresh eyes and honest instincts.
