# Two Separate Prospect Experiences

**Important:** These are two **completely unattached** evaluation experiences. Do not blend their flows or outcomes.

---

## 1. AI READINESS DIAGNOSTIC (Ladder One)

**Purpose:** Standalone prospect evaluation focused **only** on AI maturity. No service catalog, no package selection.

**Structure:** 4-rung ladder on AI:

| Rung | Name | Focus |
|------|------|--------|
| **1** | **AI Diagnostic** | Gauge awareness — where they are today (e.g. current AI IQ / gauge). |
| **2** | **Adaptation** | Identifying infrastructural adjustments needed to adopt AI. |
| **3** | **Optimization** | Complex AI chaining — advanced use and orchestration. |
| **4** | **Stewardship** | Ethics, security, bias, oversight; long-term sustainability. |

**In the build today:** Phase 1 is **Rung 1** (AI Diagnostic): public AI IQ™ snapshot, gauge, narrative, strengths/blind spots. Rungs 2–4 are planned (adaptation, optimization, stewardship).

**Do not:** Attach Socialutely service recommendations or package selection to this ladder. It is its own, closed experience.

---

## 2. ANY DOOR ASSESSMENT ENGINE

**Purpose:** Match prospect **pain points** to **Socialutely’s 22+ service solutions** and present **5 tiered packages** for the prospect to choose from. This is where commercial offers and solutions live.

**Flow (conceptual):**

1. Prospect enters via “any door” (e.g. tally-style form, chat, voice, etc.).
2. Questions similar to the **tally form** + **variables we’re building**, e.g.:
   - More revenue desired  
   - More brand awareness desired  
   - More foot / web traffic desired  
   - (Other variables TBD)
3. System uses responses + variables to **craft on demand 5 tiered packages**.
4. Prospect chooses from those packages.
5. **AI** (SMS, chat, voice, email) encourages the close.
6. **Team** supports **only if operationally necessary**.

**In the build today:** Service catalog (28 services, 10 categories, tier dots) exists. The “Any Door” engine — tally-style questions, variable set, package generator, 5 tiers, and AI/team handoff — is **not** built yet. Do not reuse the AI Readiness Diagnostic flow as the entry for this; keep a separate entry/assessment for Any Door.

---

## Summary

| Experience | Entry | Output | Attached to services? |
|------------|--------|--------|------------------------|
| **AI Readiness Diagnostic (Ladder One)** | AI diagnostic (4 rungs) | Awareness gauge → adaptation → optimization → stewardship | **No** — standalone. |
| **Any Door Assessment Engine** | Tally-like questions + variables | 5 tiered packages built from 22+ Socialutely solutions | **Yes** — packages and close. |

**Remember:**  
- **Ladder One** = AI readiness only, 4 rungs, no package/solution selection.  
- **Any Door** = pain-point + variable assessment → 5 packages → Socialutely solutions → AI + team to close.
