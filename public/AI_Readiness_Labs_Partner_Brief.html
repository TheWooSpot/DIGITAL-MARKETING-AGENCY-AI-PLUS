Partner brief

AI Readiness Labs™

Channel partner overview: four-rung curriculum, positioning, and how we co-deliver adoption outcomes with SkillSprint™ and live advisory where it matters.

Executive summary

AI Readiness Labs™ moves organizations from AI-absent to governed, operational use. Partners qualify teams, place them in the right rung, and support delivery—especially where the offer is done-with-you or long-term stewardship.

Four rungs

  • Rung 1 — Awareness: AI IQ™ assessment (live), routes to 2–4.
  • Rung 2 — Adaptation: 90-day self-guided SkillSprint Academy for scores ~0–40.
  • Rung 3 — Optimization: Done-with-you workshops for scores ~41–70.
  • Rung 4 — Stewardship: Done-for-you operating model for mature clients.
